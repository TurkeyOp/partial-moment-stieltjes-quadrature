Dear Editors,

Please consider the manuscript

“Partially Moment-Constrained Positive Stieltjes Minimax Quadrature”

for publication as an Original Paper in the Journal of Computational
and Applied Mathematics.

The manuscript develops a fixed-budget quadrature hierarchy for
integrands that combine a nearly singular Stieltjes/Lorentzian component
with an incompletely specified smooth residual. Its principal
contributions are:

1. a positive hierarchy indexed by the number of polynomial moments
   matched at infinity;
2. a difference-numerator degree bound and a corresponding
   \(2n-k+1\)-point alternation certificate within the stated class;
3. an exact coefficient-space worst-case error and model-aware
   moment-depth selector;
4. interval certification applied directly to the distributed rounded
   rules, covering 99.9703% of the tested selector domain;
5. a shifted-family stress test and a separate one-command verification
   code path.

The paper is positioned carefully relative to rationally exact
quadrature, generalized Gaussian quadrature, Markov-function
approximation, and interpolation-constrained rational minimax
approximation. We do not claim universal superiority or novelty for
those broad ideas in isolation.

The manuscript has not been published and is not under consideration
elsewhere. All authors have approved the submitted version. The
accompanying archive contains the published nodes and weights, generated
data, verification code, and scripts reproducing the principal figures
and tables.

Generative AI assistance used during manuscript preparation is disclosed
in the manuscript. All mathematical reasoning, numerical results,
references, and interpretations were reviewed and verified by the
authors, who accept full responsibility for the work.
