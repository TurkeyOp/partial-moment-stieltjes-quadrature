# Manuscript Version 2.1

## New title

**Model-Aware Positive Stieltjes Quadrature: From Structural Minimax to
Gauss-Legendre**

## Reason for the revision

Round 47 found substantial prior art for pole-aware quadrature,
generalized Gaussian exactness, positive quadrature, rational minimax,
interpolation constraints, and quadrature derived from rational
approximations. Version 2.1 therefore removes broad novelty implications.

## Primary contribution after audit

The combined framework:

- positive symmetric Stieltjes realization;
- continuum Lorentzian relative minimax;
- variable polynomial-moment depth;
- exact mixed smooth/structural selector;
- conservatively validated decision map.

The feasible-range theorem, Gaussian endpoint, alternation certificate,
and coefficient-box norm remain important supporting results but are no
longer presented as independent inventions.

## Remaining blocker

External specialist review of theorem assumptions and equivalence to
work expressed in moment-space or constrained-rational terminology.
