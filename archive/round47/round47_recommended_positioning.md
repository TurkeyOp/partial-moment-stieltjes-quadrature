# Recommended positioning for Manuscript Version 2.1

## Recommended title

**Model-Aware Positive Stieltjes Quadrature: From Structural Minimax to
Gauss-Legendre**

## One-sentence contribution

We formulate and compute, within a fixed positive symmetric Stieltjes
class, a variable-moment minimax path between a structural design and
Gauss-Legendre, and turn that path into an exact mixed-model selector
with a conservatively validated decision map.

## Claims to lead with

1. A continuum Lorentzian relative-minimax objective subject to
   positivity and a variable moment prefix.
2. An exact mixed structural/smooth risk that selects the moment depth.
3. A validated full decision map, not merely a list of quadrature rules.
4. A concise feasible-range and class-specific alternation analysis
   supporting the computation.

## Claims to subordinate

- dimension \(2n-k\);
- impossibility beyond \(2n\);
- Gauss-Legendre endpoint;
- coefficient-box dual norm;
- multi-start reconstruction.

These are important supporting results but should not be presented as
independent inventions.

## Forbidden wording before specialist confirmation

- “the first”
- “never previously studied”
- “unprecedented”
- “a fundamentally new theory”
- “global uniqueness”
- “formal certification”
- “universally superior”

## Defensible wording

- “within the stated positive symmetric class”
- “to the best of our targeted literature search”
- “we did not identify a direct match”
- “class-specific alternation certificate”
- “conservative numerical validation”
- “local robustness under the tested shift box”
