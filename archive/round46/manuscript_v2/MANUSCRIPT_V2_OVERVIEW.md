**From Structural Minimax to Gauss-Legendre: A Positive

## Core scientific change

Version 1 treated \(0\leq k\leq n\) as the hierarchy. Version 2 proves
that the complete feasible range within the positive symmetric class is

\[
0\leq k\leq2n,
\qquad
\dim\mathcal A_{n,k}=2n-k,
\]

with \(k=2n\) equal to the \(2n\)-point Gauss-Legendre rule and
\(k>2n\) impossible.
