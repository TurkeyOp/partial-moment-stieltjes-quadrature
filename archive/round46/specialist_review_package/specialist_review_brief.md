# Specialist review brief

## Manuscript

**From Structural Minimax to Gauss-Legendre: A Positive
Moment-Constrained Stieltjes Quadrature Hierarchy**

Author: Tran Minh Duc, FPT University.

## Reason for review

The manuscript has undergone a major theoretical expansion. The main
question is no longer whether a five-rule partial hierarchy works.
The revised claim is that, within the stated positive symmetric class,
the full feasible range is \(0\leq k\leq2n\), with dimension
\(2n-k\) and Gauss-Legendre as the maximal endpoint.

## Evidence supplied

- Manuscript Version 2 PDF and LaTeX source.
- Full hierarchy nodes, weights, and alternation points.
- Validated full-selector data (97.9067% assigned area).
- Multi-start reconstruction summary for six upper-half problems.
- Claim-evidence matrix and focused review questions.

## Requested expertise

Numerical quadrature, rational/Markov/Stieltjes approximation,
Chebyshev systems, or nonlinear minimax approximation.
